Trout has been with Homeless Tails since he was 5 days old. Born on the street to a mom who was one of the skinniest kitties they have ever met, his life did not look too promising. But big doses of daily love and tender care helped him become the amazingly sweet kitty he is today! He had turned into an incredible lap cat when in foster. Trout loves to play and purr nonstop; even welcoming a feline friend or two.
