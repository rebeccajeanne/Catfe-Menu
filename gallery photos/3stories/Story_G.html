Sweet, calm Dallas is waiting for his forever home! At the Catfe he keeps to himself— this gentle soul would much rather be the king of a quiet home or part of a smaller, peaceful pack. He’s playful too, when he doesn’t have to compete for toys.

Dallas was surrendered to the Paterson pound, and one persons trash is another person’s treasure. He may have been tossed aside, but to us he’s pure gold. He is front-declawed (ouch), so we’re extra grateful he’s safe inside now. If your home needs calm energy and a beautiful, soothing presence, Dallas might be the gentleman you’ve been searching for.
