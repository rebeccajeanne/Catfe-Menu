Cagney and Lacey (ear-tip and all!) — the perfect pair and partners in purrs! These sweet, playful girls started their life struggling to survive within a feral colony in Newark, and when they were trapped for TNR (Trap, Neuter, and Release), they were just too loving to let go! Now they’re thriving indoors and soaking up every bit of affection. They are ready for their very first home and family. Mellow, affectionate, and full of heart, Lacey is ready to start her happily ever after with someone who will treasure her as much as she’ll treasure you.
